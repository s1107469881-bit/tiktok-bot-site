Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

1. Introduction

My Content Bot ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our content automation service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • TikTok Account Information: Access tokens and account identifiers necessary to publish content on your behalf via the TikTok Content Posting API.
  • Content Data: Video scripts, voiceover text, and metadata generated or provided for content creation.
  • Usage Logs: Records of content published, including timestamps and publication status.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information solely to:

  • Authenticate with TikTok on your behalf to publish content
  • Generate and schedule video content as configured by you
  • Maintain logs of published content for your review

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties.

4. TikTok API Usage

This application uses the TikTok Content Posting API. By using this service, you authorize us to access your TikTok account solely for the purpose of publishing content on your behalf. We only request the minimum permissions necessary (video.publish and video.upload scopes).

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. API tokens are stored securely and are never shared with unauthorized parties.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You may also revoke TikTok API access at any time through your TikTok account settings.
